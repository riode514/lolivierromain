Our Rooms

Four unique sanctuaries of comfort

La Lavande

A serene sanctuary where purple hues and the gentle scent of lavender create an atmosphere of pure tranquility. The room opens to views of the garden's herb borders, with original stone walls and a private en-suite bathroom featuring vintage fixtures.

Amenities: En-suite bathroom, Garden view, Original stone walls, Premium linens

Le Figuier

Beneath the shade of ancient fig trees, this room captures the essence of Provence with warm terracotta tones and rustic wooden beams that tell stories of generations past. French doors open to a private terrace overlooking the courtyard.

Amenities: Private terrace, Exposed beams, En-suite bathroom, Courtyard view

L'Amande

Soft almond whites and natural textures create a cocoon of comfort. French doors open to a private terrace where morning coffee becomes a sacred ritual. The room features original terracotta floors and a beautifully appointed bathroom.

Amenities: Private terrace, Terracotta floors, En-suite bathroom, Morning sun exposure

La Pierre

Honoring the ancient stonework of Provence, this room blends original architecture with contemporary luxury, creating a timeless retreat for the soul. Features include vaulted stone ceilings and a spa-like bathroom with natural stone details.

Amenities: Vaulted ceilings, Stone details, Spa-like bathroom, Pool view
