Your Host

Meet Neil Clegg

Neil Clegg

Meet Neil Clegg, a British host who fell in love with the art de vivre of the South of France. With a deep appreciation for design, comfort, and detail, Neil has curated a haven where every moment feels effortless and warm.

His immaculate attention and local insight ensure your stay in the Uzège region is nothing short of exceptional. From restaurant recommendations to hidden gems known only to locals, Neil's guidance will enrich your Provençal experience.

Philosophy of Hospitality

Neil believes that true hospitality goes beyond providing a beautiful space—it's about creating an atmosphere where guests feel genuinely welcomed and cared for. Having spent over a decade in the region, he has developed deep relationships with local artisans, restaurateurs, and cultural sites that allow him to offer authentic insights into Provençal life.

His approach is both professional and personal: anticipating needs before they arise while respecting each guest's desire for privacy and independence. Whether you seek recommendations for the perfect bistro, guidance to a hidden viewpoint, or simply someone who understands the local rhythm, Neil provides the perfect balance of expertise and discretion.

How Neil Enhances Your Stay

Local Insights

Benefit from Neil's decade of local knowledge with personalized recommendations for restaurants, markets, cultural sites, and hidden gems.

Concierge Services

From restaurant reservations to wine tasting appointments, Neil handles the details so you can focus on enjoying your stay.

Cultural Guidance

Navigate the rich history and traditions of the region with insights into local customs, festivals, and cultural experiences.

Attention to Detail

Every aspect of your stay is thoughtfully considered, from room preparation to ensuring your comfort throughout your visit.

Privacy & Respect

While always available when needed, Neil respects your space and independence, ensuring you feel at home rather than watched.

Seamless Experience

From arrival to departure, Neil orchestrates a seamless experience that allows you to immerse fully in the magic of Provence.

A Personal Approach

What sets Neil apart is his genuine passion for sharing the beauty and culture of his adopted home. He understands that each guest arrives with different interests and desires, and he takes time to understand what will make your stay most meaningful.

Whether you're interested in the historical significance of Roman monuments, the intricacies of local wine-making, the best times to visit quiet chapels, or simply where to find the most perfect café au lait, Neil's enthusiasm and knowledge transform a good holiday into an unforgettable experience.

His dedication to excellence has earned consistent praise from guests who return year after year, not just for the beauty of L'Oliver Romain, but for the warmth and expertise that Neil brings to every interaction.
